## MoSCoW Prioritisation

### Must Have (MVP)

- **5 Areas Model** — The foundational CBT framework used in session 1 across every program and every tier. Patient maps a situation across 5 areas (Situation, Thoughts, Emotions, Behaviours, Physical symptoms), then reflects on how to break the cycle. The proposal specifies this for self-help, CBT-guided, and PWP-guided tiers. This is the single most important unbuilt feature. Two consecutive PM briefs have recommended it as next.
- **General Goals** — Therapy goal-setting with 0-10 ratings at start, mid, and end of therapy. Used from session 1. The proposal specifies reflection prompts ("Are these goals in line with your values?", "Are they achievable?"). Multiple goals supported.
- **Weekly Goals** — Weekly to-do list tied to the Activity Diary. Patients set behavioural goals, tick them off, reflect with prompt questions. Creates the homework compliance loop that makes guided CBT work.
- **Psychoeducation presenter** — Module type exists in BE with content. Patients assigned psychoeducation modules currently hit a dead end. Needed for Panic Disorder program and introductory material across all programs.

### Should Have (Post-MVP, High Priority)

- **Thinking Traps** — Learn and identify cognitive distortions. Sessions 4-6 of Depression program. High clinical value but not needed for early sessions.
- **Thought Monitoring** — Log unhelpful thoughts and tag with thinking traps. Depends on Thinking Traps.
- **Thought Challenging** — Structured evidence for/against worksheet. Core cognitive restructuring tool. Depends on Thought Monitoring.
- **Exercise presenter** — Module type exists in BE but no FE presenter. Lower urgency than psychoeducation.
- **Password change / account settings** — Basic account management. Not blocking therapy workflow but expected by users.

### Could Have (Future)

- **Longitudinal Model** — Beck formulation. Advanced, used once or twice per therapy course.
- **Belief Change Evidence Table** — Late-stage cognitive work. Depends on Thought Challenging.
- **Modifying Unhelpful Beliefs** — Structured worksheet. Same dependency chain.
- **Self-help program recommendation** — Suggest programs from questionnaire scores. Valuable for self-help tier.
- **Additional programs** (GAD, Panic, OCD, etc.) — Depression must work completely first.
- **Recurring assignment automation** — BE has recurrence data model but no scheduler. Therapists currently re-assign manually.

### Won't Have (This Cycle)

- **PWP tier differentiation** — Shares tools with CBT tier. Defer until CBT tier is complete.
- **Self-help tier** — Business partners prioritised therapist-guided.
- **ACT, Sleep, Assertiveness programs** — Secondary programs.
- **PTSD, Agoraphobia, Social Anxiety** — Require specialist adaptations.
- **Notification system** — Infrastructure that doesn't deliver clinical value yet.

## Recommendation

### 1. Build the 5 Areas Model (top priority)

This is the third consecutive PM brief recommending this feature. The polish cycle is done — there are no more blockers. This is the highest-value, highest-impact tool to build:

- Used in **every** CBT program, in **session 1**, across **all three tiers**
- Self-contained — no dependencies on other unbuilt tools
- Unlocks a meaningful first therapy session (5 Areas + Activity Diary + PHQ-9)
- The proposal provides clear requirements: fill in 5 areas for a situation, reflect on breaking the cycle, save for therapist review
- Needs: BE model + controller, shared types, FE presenter component, FE hook

### 2. Build General Goals immediately after

Together with the 5 Areas Model, this covers sessions 1-2 of a Depression program. The proposal specifies:
- Goal text + current rating (0-10) + mid-therapy rating + end-of-therapy rating
- Multiple goals supported
- Reflection prompts ("Are these goals achievable?", "Are they in line with your values?")
- 6-week reminder to re-rate

### 3. Build Psychoeducation presenter as a smaller task between or alongside

This is lower-complexity (read-only content display) and unblocks an entire module type. Can be slotted in as a palate cleanser between the larger clinical tool builds.

### What NOT to build yet

- **Weekly Goals** — Natural follow-on after General Goals, but don't jump ahead
- **Thinking Traps / Thought tools** — Mid-therapy. Sessions 4-6.
- **New programs** — Depression must work end-to-end first
- **Profile/settings** — Nice to have, not blocking therapy workflow
